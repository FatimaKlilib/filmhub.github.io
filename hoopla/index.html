The Process

4 Simple Steps

  1. Sign up for a free Kinonation account in 2 minutes.
  2. Upload your video titles. Big catalog? Send hard drive(s).
  3. Enter metadata & upload artwork for your titles
  4. Select hoopla digital as one of your VOD platforms

You can also select other VOD platforms like Hulu, Google Play, M-GO, Vimeo... at no extra cost.

Automated Distribution

Quality Control (QC)

Once we have all required assets (main video, trailer, metadata, artwork, English subtitles or closed captions) it will pass through Kinonation quality control (QC) to ensure your the title meets hoopla's delivery spec.

Selection by hoopla

Hoopla is a curated platform, meaning they select only those titles that match their editorial and programming policy & objectives; hoopla will decline titles at their discretion.

Delivery

Once your film or TV content passes QC the Kinonation content ops team will immediately trigger custom transcode & metadata authoring exactly to the delivery specs for hoopla digital. All assets will be delivered to the hoopla content management system.

Live Date

The timeline from delivery to "content live" is subject to hoopla's content ingest team, editorial timelines, etc.

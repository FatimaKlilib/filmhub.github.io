Upload-Specs Condensed Overview

Summary

Apple ProRes or Avid DNxHD .mov, stereo audio, no letterbox - interlaced - preroll - postroll - URLs

Official specs, detailed info and support in our help center article.

Accepted

Codec
Apple ProRes 422 HQ, Avid DNxHD (8 bit)
Frame rate
Any progressive, examples: 23.98p, 24p, 25p, 30p, 29.97p
File Size
Unlimited (200 GB+). The bigger the better.
File Format
QuickTime .mov
Fast Start
Enabled
Resolution
Minimum SD (480 height). Preferred HD (720 width or more), 2k, 4k.
Pixel Aspect Ratio
Square pixels only
Letterbox
No black bars around the picture
Pre/Post Roll
None. No slate, slug, bars, tone, cards. Max 1 sec black before & after.
Audio
PCM Stereo, single stream. No surround yet, sorry
Subtitles
Separate SRT file. No burn-in. More on Closed Captions and Subtitles.

Not accepted

Letterbox
No
Pillarbox
No
Interlaced
No
Subtitles
No burn in except trailers
Anamorphic pixels
No anamorphic ("squeezed") pixels. Square pixels only.
Websites
No URLs or promo links (e.g. film website, Facebook page, available to download, etc.) anywhere in the film or trailer, including end credits
MPAA card
No - neither real nor bogus
Other No's
No bars, slate, test-tone, pre-roll, post-roll (max. 1 sec of black before film starts or after credits end)
Title-Safe
No titles or captions outside title-safe area

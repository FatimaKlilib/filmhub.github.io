How it works

1You upload

Upload your film (TV show, short etc.), trailer, artwork and enter metadata using our unique Upload Manager. We support resume upload, parallel upload from several locations (for example from your office, home and post facility at the same time - make the upload fly), and unlimited file size.

2We author

Your video assets are transcoded into many formats as required by our digital outlets, for example iTunes, Netflix, Amazon, Hulu and many more. Our system also prepares the artwork and "metadata" files which describe your title. Then we package everything into outlet-specific delivery bundles.

3We distribute

Your title - including metadata, trailer & images - is automatically delivered to the outlets you chose. They review the package and make their selection.

4 They watch

Consumers rent, buy or stream your film to their HDTVs, home theater systems, game consoles, Blu-Ray players, streaming players, computers, phones and tablets.

5 You get paid

We consolidate accounting from all outlets, and create convenient online reporting for you. The outlets pay us, we keep our 20% share, and you can withdraw your revenue anytime.

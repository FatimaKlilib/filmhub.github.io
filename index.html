How it works

1 You upload

Upload your film (TV show, short etc.), trailer, artwork and enter metadata using our unique Upload Manager. We support features like resume upload, parallel upload from several locations (for example, from your office, home and post facility at the same time, to make the upload fly), and unlimited file size.

2 We author

Your film is transcoded into all the different formats as required by our digital outlets, for example iTunes, Netflix, Amazon, Hulu, Viewster and many more. Our system also prepares the artwork and specific "metadata" files which describe your title. Then we package everything into a nice delivery bundle, once for each outlet.

3 We distribute

Your title - including metadata, trailer & images - is automatically delivered to the outlets you choose. They review the film package and decide whether to select (or not) for their platform.

4 They watch

Consumers rent, buy or stream your film to their HDTVs, home theater systems, game consoles, Blu-Ray players, streaming players, computers, phones and tablets.

5 You get paid

We consolidate accounting from all outlets, and create convenient online reporting for you. The outlets pay us, we keep our 20% share, and you can withdraw your revenue anytime.

Kinonation - Brief Startup Profile

Kinonation is a technology startup reimagining the film distribution industry. A scalable cloud-based web app directly connects filmmakers with streaming video services like Amazon and Hulu, eliminating all middlemen including sales agents, distributors, aggregators and encoding labs. The opportunity is a meaningful share of the $62 billion worldwide streaming video market.

Current status:

  • kinonation.com live and in public beta
  • $500,000 revenue
  • 4500 titles catalog
  • $700,000 seed funding-to-date
  • streaming partners include Amazon, Hulu, M-Go, Vimeo and China Telecom in 12 territories

Kinonation operates entirely in the Amazon AWS cloud and develops using agile methodologies and modern frameworks including Rails 5 and React.JS.
